Human-Centered Design/UX Design

2015

User experience design, prototyping an app that helps people consider energy use when buying large appliances. Performed needfinding with users in their homes and at point-of-sale locations. Responsible for the appliances module within the greater context of the Howpower project, part of the ARPA-E funded Energy Behaviour initiative at Stanford.

The design features friendly, accessible language rendered in a serif typeface. The application is structured to follow the buying process, and provides process guidance at each step. Most importantly, it features a persistent efficiency range comparison expressed in annual operating cost, an approach supported by research to positively bias buying decisions toward energy efficient products.
