Product Design: Wireless Charging Prototype

2016 - Current

Functional prototype for a product that sets out to redefine our relationship with personal technology. Based on wireless charging technology, the intent is to make power-hungry devices more wearable, flexible, and transparent by liberating them from the constraints imposed by battery density.

Reimagining the casing as a pocketable object, the device is cloaked in premium New Zealand wool.
Soft closures at each end of the device provide cushioning necessary to have the device sit comfortably in a variety of different pocket geometries.
