About

Stanford MS '15 | Canterbury BEng (Hons, First Class) '11
Born and raised in New Zealand, and now living in Boston. I was originally inspired to work in buildings and risk following the devastation of the Canterbury earthquakes that coincided with my studies in 2010-11. Now I’m an analyst and designer who has worked across product, software, risk, and built environment projects.

I have had the privilege of working with companies and organizations like Apple, Google, WeWork, Foster and Partners, Bjarke Ingels Group, Snøhetta, Heatherwick Studio, Arup, Port of Portland, the University of British Columbia, EnergyNest, Katerra, RAD Urban and more.
