Glass Structures: Transparent Pavilion

2015

Engineering leadership role on this glass pavilion project, designed and fabricated in collaboration with Professor Jun Sato (Tokyo University), and Stanford Architecture. The structure is comprised of self-stable modules of thin rectangular glass plates. AGC supplied flexible chemically strengthened glass for the project, the main structure of which makes use of 1-2mm thick Dragontrail and Leoflex panels.
